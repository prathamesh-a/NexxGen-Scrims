About Us

Nexx-Gen SCRIMS is a website designed to help new and upcoming gamers in India. In recent times, gaming has seen an influx in terms of gamers choosing it as a career choice. Many such gamers have the required skill to excel, but no ideal base for showing their excellence. Our website is aimed at providing tournaments for such gamers, where entry is free of cost, unlike some other websites, where entry fees are charged. We aim to have clean and smooth hosting of various tournaments of various games. Currently, games for which tournaments can be participated in are: BGMI India Mobile, League of Legends, Valorant, and FIFA.
